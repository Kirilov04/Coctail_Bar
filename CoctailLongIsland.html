Ingredients

3-Mile Long Island Iced Tea

  • 1/2 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Light rum
  • 1/2 oz Tequila
  • 1/2 oz Triple sec
  • 1/2 oz Vodka
  • 1/2oz Coca-Cola
  • 1-2 dash Sweet and sour
  • 1 wedge Biters
  • Garnish with Lemon

Instructions

Fill 14oz glass with ice and alcohol. Fill 2/3 glass with cola and remainder with sweet & sour. Top with dash of bitters and lemon wedge..
