Ingredients

A Day at the Beach

  • 1 oz Coconut rum
  • 1/2 oz Amaretto
  • 4 oz Orange juice
  • 1/2 oz Grenadine

Instructions

Shake Rum, Amaretto, and Orange Juice in a shaker filled with ice. Strain over ice into a highball glass. Add Grenadine and garnish with a Pineapple Wedge and a Strawberry.

Glass

Serve: Highball glass
